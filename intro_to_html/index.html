The Breakfast Club

The Breakfast Club is a movie about 5 teenagers at a highschool who all get served detention on a saturday. They all come from different cliques, but throughout the movie they make friends with eachother and decide that even though they are from different friend groups, they will continue to be friends even though they made be considered "weird".

Characters

John Bender, Allison Reynolds, Claire Standish, Brian Johnson, Andrew Clark, Carl the Janitor, Richard Vernon
